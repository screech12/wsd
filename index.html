The Pros and the Cons

What are the pros of introducing ipad into the classroom? Could it be a few less books to carry each day? Fewer books would mean less to carry each day to school and easier on your back. Children that do not have access to computers will learn how to use them which will increase their ability and preparing them for real world use of applications. They will also have access to many programs that they would not normal be able to afford or use at home. The use of the IPad will encourage students and create curiosity towards learning. Games and programs will allow for not only seeing, hearing but also applying the learned activities. There seems to be an app for everything these days, and the number of educational apps just keeps growing.
